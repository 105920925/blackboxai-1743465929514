Resume Writing Guide

1. Contact Information

Include your name, phone number, email, and LinkedIn profile at the top.

2. Professional Summary

Write a 3-4 sentence overview of your skills and experience.

3. Work Experience

  • List jobs in reverse chronological order
  • Use bullet points for responsibilities
  • Quantify achievements when possible

4. Education

Include degrees, certifications, and relevant coursework.
